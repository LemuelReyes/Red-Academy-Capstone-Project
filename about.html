"The flag of honesty, integrity, and excellence staked on immigration" is the guiding principle by which Filmex Immigration Consulting Agency operates ever since it was founded in 2009 by ICCRC and CAPIC member Elvira Gangte. Filmex has since maintained it's exceptional, reliable, and professional Immigration Services even up to this day.

Mrs. Elvira Gangte / ICCRC Member Membership Number: ICCRC R410135 is the principal professional Immigration Consultant for Filmex Immigration. She is a certified member of Immigration Consultants of Canadian Regulatory Council (ICCRC), which is the regulatory body for authorized, qualified, and recognized Immigration Consultants. ICCRC regulates the Registered Immigration Consultants of Canada. They ensure that regulated members conduct their professional business within the legal boundaries of Canadian Law and human rights. In essence they protect the clients from unlicensed, unrecognized fraudulent scammers who disguise themselves as Immigration Consultants.

CAPIC MEMBER Membership Number: R14179 Elvira is also a member of the Canadian Association of Professional Immigration Consultants (CAPIC) R14179. While ICCRC monitors Immigration Consultants and their activities for the benefit of the applicants; CAPIC represents and protects registered Immigration Consultant members. This professional organization updates its members on the changing Immigration laws, policies, regulations, processes implemented by Citizenship and Immigration Canada (CIC).
